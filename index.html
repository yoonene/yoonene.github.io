About

I care about AI systems that hold up in production

I'm most comfortable working where model quality meets product reality: retrieval that has to return the right thing, serving that has to stay fast under load, and evaluation that helps teams make better decisions instead of guessing.

Most of my recent work has been in game AI products, where search quality, latency, cost, and user experience all matter at the same time.
